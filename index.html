The life and experiences of Maggie

Hello, I'm Maggie!

Aren't I cute? I'm a West Highland White Terrier living my best live with my furever family in the Greater St. Louis area. Life wasn't always this great though...

Life Before Adoption

I spent the first four years of my life with an animal hoarder. I can't quite remember how many other animals lived with me, but I will tell you that at least nine of them were cats. Unfortunately, I'm allegic to cats and unable to control myself around their food. As my former family added more and more cats to our home, I lost my fur and broke out in a rash that escalated to a skin infection. Finally, a relative took my from my former family, and placed me in the care of Partners for Pets.

Adoption

Living in an animal shelter was a welcome change. I had plenty of new friends, and I wasnt allergic to any of them! With medical treatment and the absence of feline roommates, my skin healed, and my fur started to grow back. I was only at the shelter for a few weeks before I was was introduced to the people who would become my furever family. The day that they officially got to take me home was extremely busy, but I didn't mind! I got to meet my sister, who was not interested in meeting me at all, but I loved her! The rest of the family decided that we would get to know each other better over time. This is definitely true, but she still does not enjoy playing with me.

Getting Used to My New Family

Getting used to my new family was not as easy as I thought it would be. All the smells where new, and it took me a while to find my favorite spots to relax. I love to lay in the sun, and it took me FOREVER to find the best indoor spots to do that. My sister was exceptionally territorial, and the sibling rivalry was (and still is) very real!

My Sister, Roxie

Speaking of my sister, here she is! This is my sister, Roxie. We definitely still have an interesting love/hate relationship, but it works.
